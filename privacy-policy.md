Privacy Policy for Flow Studio - Batch Manager
Last updated: April 17, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Flow Studio - Batch Manager ("Extension", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. Our core philosophy is simple: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data to any external servers.

1. Information We Collect
A. Non-Collection of Personal Data
The Extension operates entirely locally within your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, usage metrics, or analytics to any servers that we control.

B. Clipboard Data
The Extension requests the clipboardRead permission. This is strictly used to enhance the user experience by allowing you to paste reference images or prompt text directly into the extension's interface.

The clipboard data is only read when you perform a copy-paste action within the extension.
The data is processed locally and is never sent to any third-party servers except directly to the official Google Labs API when you initiate a video/image generation workflow.
C. Local Storage
The Extension uses your browser's local storage capabilities (via the storage permission) to save your settings, preferences, API keys (if applicable), and task queue layouts. This data is stored strictly on your local device and is not synchronized to or accessible by the developer.

D. Third-Party Interactions
The Extension acts as an automation bridge between your prompts and the Google Labs Flow platform. When you use the Extension, your data (prompts and generated content) is communicated directly between your browser and https://labs.google. We do not act as a middleman in this data transfer. Your data handling during this process is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information
Any data processed by this Extension (such as prompts or reference images) is used solely to execute the generation requests that you explicitly initiate. It is immediately discarded or stored locally based on your preferences.

3. Data Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or rent any personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Access
If you provide API keys (such as OpenAI or Gemini API keys) within the Extension to generate prompts, these keys are stored securely in your local browser storage. They are used only to make direct, secure API calls to the respective providers' official endpoints. We cannot access your API keys.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

6. Contact Us
If you have questions or comments about this policy, or if you need to request the deletion of your local data (which you can do manually by clearing your browser's extension data), please contact us at: linhbp0808@gmail.com
